Surviving the First Night

To survive in Minecraft, you must follow a strict order of operations:

  1. Punch a tree to gather wood logs.
  2. Open your inventory to craft planks.
  3. Create a Crafting Table.
    • Requires: 4 Wood Planks
    • Unlocks: 3x3 Crafting Grid
  4. Craft a Wooden Pickaxe.
  5. Mine Stone to upgrade your tools.
